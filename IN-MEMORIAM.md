# In memory of José Antonio Rodríguez (1960–2022)

Astro-Nex was created by **José Antonio Rodríguez** — its author, its developer, and
the mind behind every calculation it makes. He built it to draw astrological charts in
the API method of Bruno and Louise Huber, and he shared it freely with the world.

Original project: **https://astro-nex.net/**

This repository exists to honor and continue his work. The modernization to Python 3
and GTK 3 changes only the foundations it stands on — the design, the method, the
insight, and the care are entirely his. Everything here descends from his Astro-Nex.

All recognition for this software belongs to him. Its maintenance and its future
improvements are offered in his memory.

**A su honor — this work, its maintenance, and its improvements.**

*Que su trabajo siga vivo.*
